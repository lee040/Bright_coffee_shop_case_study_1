****** OVERVIEW OF THE CASE STUDY ******

. The Bright coffee shop project analyzes transactional data from a coffee shop chain to uncover sales patterns, revenue drivers and customer behaviour insight that can help improve operations and marketing strategies.

******* DATA ANALYSIS AND INSIGHTS *******

. Data collection and cleaning - Raw transaction records were imported into a Databricks database and cleaned for consistency.
. Analysis - SQL queries aggregated sales by day, time of day , product type, and store location, driving metrics like daily revenue ,number of sale, and product popularity.
. Insights - key findings include peak purchase times ,best selling product categories and revenue variations between weekday and weekend sales.
. Recommendations - optimize staffing during peak hours, promote specific categories in low performing times and tailor marketing for weekend and weekday customers.

****** SUMMARY AND INSIGHTS *********

. Built a SQL based analytics framework to process coffee shop transactions.
.Generated detailed daily sales reports with time_of_day and price range segmentation.
. Identified operational and marketing opportunity to boost revenue and customer experiance.
